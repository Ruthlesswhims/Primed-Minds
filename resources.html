Teaching Materials

To complement Primed Minds Explorations, we have developed Teacher Resources to help teachers plan lessons and extension materials related to content in our Explorations. These Teacher Resources are being road tested in over 100 schools throughout Australia (in conjunction with Maths Pathway – link) and the US. Click here to download a sample lesson plan.

Interested in trying our Teacher’s Resources? Email us at: teachers@primedminds.com
